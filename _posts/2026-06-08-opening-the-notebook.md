This blog will collect compact notes on research directions, implementation details, and ideas I want to make more concrete over time. I expect many of the posts to sit near the same questions as my current work: how AI systems search, when they fail to ground answers, and how we can build models and tools that are easier to inspect.

I also want this space to be useful as a builder's log. That means notes from experiments, practical lessons from projects, and occasional writeups on the engineering choices behind tools I ship.
