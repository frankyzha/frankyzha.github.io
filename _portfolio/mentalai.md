Led a team of 4 to fine-tune GPT-4o mini on datasets of conversations between psychologists and patients to provide mental health support. I re-engineered prompts to maintain the context window, collaborated on the frontend in React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, used MongoDB for storage, and integrated the OpenAI API for real-time model responses.
